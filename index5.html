Poems for Ariane

Eternal Dance

In your gaze, stars twinkle bright,
Our love, a symphony of pure delight.
Hand in hand, through life we prance,
An eternal dance, our hearts entrance.

With every heartbeat, our connection deep,
In love's garden, our promises we keep.
Forever entwined, our souls enhance,
An endless ballet, our love's sweet romance.

Moonlit Whispers

Beneath the moon, our shadows blend,
In your eyes, a love that has no end.
Soft whispers linger, a tender chance,
Our hearts entwined, a timeless dance.

Through the night, stars spell our story,
In your arms, I find eternal glory.
Forevermore, in this love expanse,
Our souls entangled, a divine romance.

Forever Yours

In your laughter, my world takes flight,
Your touch, a warmth in the coldest night.
Hand in hand, through life we explore,
Forever yours, today and evermore.

In your eyes, reflections of our dreams,
Love's symphony, in heartbeats it teems.
Together we dance, an eternal shore,
Forever yours, today and evermore.

With you, every moment finds its grace,
In your embrace, I've found my place.
Through life's twists, our love will endure,
Forever yours, today and evermore.

Serenade of the Heart

As I gaze into your eyes so deep,
A love unfolds, like secrets to keep.
Wild and free, like a summer breeze,
In your love, I find my heart at ease.

With each breath, your love draws near,
A warm embrace, erasing every fear.
Deep and real, like a favorite song,
With you, my love, where I belong.

You're my sun, my moon, my guiding light,
In life's stormy sea, you hold me tight.
Through darkest hours, you're my spark,
My love, my heart, in you, I embark.

In Your Presence

Without you, life's a lonely fate,
Endless days, a daunting weight.
No hand to hold, no love to find,
In darkness, I'd be left behind.

But with you, my dear, the world transforms,
Loneliness fades, replaced by warm norms.
Days are painted with laughter and light,
Your love, a beacon in my darkest night.

Your presence, a melody of grace,
Every shared moment, a tender embrace.
Together, we conquer the challenges we face,
In the race of life, we find our pace.

Without you, a path unknown and bare,
With you, life's burdens become easier to bear.
My heart sings with joy, in love we ensue,
Life is brighter, truer, with you.

Starlit Bond

You appeared in my life, a radiant star,
A source of joy that traveled from afar.
My pain, you shouldered, a burden you bore,
A love so deep, like none before.

With your smile, life gained a new hue,
Making each day worthwhile, shared with you.
Through highs and lows, hand in hand we'd stand,
In the dance of love, our hearts expand.

Bright Transformation

Before you entered my existence,
all was shrouded in darkness and despair.
My life lacked the grace of clouds and sunlight,
as well as the beauty of stars in the night air.

I never held faith in enchantment,
yet you materialized, a wondrous sight.
I once anticipated a solitary journey,
but now, together, we form a perfect light.

You shape the essence of my being,
sculpting me in every conceivable way.
Each moment is precious, a testament,
as I willingly dedicate my entire day.

Our connection is intense,
a fierce flame for all to witness.
The emotions exchanged between us,
a reciprocity of love, profound and limitless.
